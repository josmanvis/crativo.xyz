1. **Deep Dives** — Technical breakdowns (1500-2000 words)

3. **Tools** — What I built and why (800-1200 words)
4. **Career** — Lessons and reflections (600-1000 words)
5. **Quick Tips** — Short, actionable posts (300-500 words)
